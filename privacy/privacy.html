Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect much. We use cookies for ads and analytics. You can opt out. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws worldwide.

1. Introduction

Welcome to DontWork.work ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior, including:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, device type, operating system
  • Location Data: General geographic location (country/region) based on IP address
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser version, time zone, referring website

2.2 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a contact form
  • Subscribe to our newsletter (if applicable)
  • Participate in surveys or promotions

This may include: name, email address, and message content.

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns (Analytics)
  • Deliver personalized advertisements (Advertising)
  • Remember your preferences (Functionality)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain our website
  • Improve user experience and site functionality
  • Analyze usage trends and optimize content
  • Display relevant advertisements (via third-party ad networks)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure website security

4. Third-Party Services

We may share your information with third-party service providers, including:

4.1 Analytics Providers

  • Cloudflare Analytics (or similar): Traffic analysis and performance monitoring
  • Plausible/Google Analytics (if applicable): User behavior and engagement metrics

4.2 Advertising Networks

  • Google Adsense, Ezoic, Mediavine, or similar ad networks: Display personalized advertisements
  • Amazon Associates: Affiliate marketing program

4.3 Contact Form Service

  • Tally.so: Contact form submissions and email delivery

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object: Opt out of data processing for marketing purposes
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent at any time

5.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know: Request information about data we collect, use, and share
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of your personal information
  • Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

Note: We do not sell personal information in the traditional sense, but sharing data with ad networks may be considered a "sale" under CCPA.

5.3 Other Jurisdictions

Users in other regions (including China, Canada, Australia, etc.) may have similar rights under local laws. Contact us to exercise your rights.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Analytics data is typically retained for 24-36 months. Contact form submissions are retained indefinitely unless you request deletion.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Use our website at your own risk.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this policy and applicable law.

10. Cookies and Opt-Out Options

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block All Cookies: Set your browser to refuse all cookies (may limit site functionality)
  • Delete Cookies: Clear cookies from your browser history
  • Opt Out of Interest-Based Ads: Visit Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out or Your Online Choices (EU)

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us:

  • Email: Via our Contact Form
  • Website: https://dontwork.work

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to transparency and protecting your data. If you have concerns or questions, don't hesitate to reach out.
